Complete Guide to DBT Seeding

Follow these simple steps to enable DBT seeding on your bank account and ensure your scholarship reaches you without any delays

⚠️ Important Information

  • Your bank account must be DBT-enabled and Aadhaar seeded
  • Simply having Aadhaar linked to your account is NOT sufficient for scholarship payments
  • DBT seeding enables your account to receive government benefits and scholarships
  • The account holder name should match exactly with Aadhaar
  • Ensure your mobile number is updated in both Aadhaar and bank records

Step-by-Step Process

1

Visit Your Bank Branch

Go to the branch where you have your savings account. Carry the following documents:

  • Original Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • Identity Proof (if name differs)
  • Mobile phone for OTP verification
2

Request for DBT Seeding

Inform the bank official that you want to:

  • Enable DBT seeding on your bank account
  • Link your Aadhaar number for DBT transactions
  • Verify that your account can receive government benefits and scholarships
  • Ensure your account is DBT-enabled for scholarship payments
3

Fill the DBT Seeding Form

The bank will provide you with a DBT seeding application form. Fill in:

  • Your complete bank account number
  • 12-digit Aadhaar number
  • Updated mobile number
  • Request for DBT enablement specifically
  • Sign the form as per your bank signature
4

OTP Verification

Complete the verification process:

  • Bank will send OTP to your Aadhaar-registered mobile
  • Provide the OTP to the bank official
  • Wait for the system confirmation
  • Collect the acknowledgment receipt
5

Verification & Confirmation

Ensure the process is completed successfully:

  • Ask for written confirmation from the bank
  • Note down the reference number if provided
  • Verify your account status online after 2-3 days
  • Keep the acknowledgment safe for future reference

Alternative Methods

🏧 ATM Method

Some banks allow Aadhaar seeding through ATM:

  • Insert your debit card and enter PIN
  • Select "Aadhaar Services" option
  • Choose "Link Aadhaar Number"
  • Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number
  • Complete OTP verification

Note: This facility may not be available in all banks.

📱 Online/Mobile Banking

Link through your bank's online portal:

  • Login to your net banking account
  • Look for "Aadhaar Services" or "Link Aadhaar"
  • Enter your Aadhaar number
  • Verify through OTP
  • Save the confirmation details

Note: Available only in select banks with this feature.

How to Verify Your DBT Status

📧 SMS Method

Send SMS: UIDAI STATE your_aadhaar_number to 51969

You will receive confirmation of linked bank accounts

🌐 Online Verification

Visit: resident.uidai.gov.in

Use "Aadhaar Services" to check bank account linkage

🏦 Bank Statement

Request updated bank statement showing Aadhaar seeding status

Look for "Aadhaar Linked: Yes" mention

Common Issues & Solutions

Name Mismatch Between Aadhaar and Bank Account

+

Solution: Update your name in either Aadhaar or bank account to match exactly. Minor spelling differences can cause rejection.

  • For Aadhaar name change: Visit Aadhaar center with supporting documents
  • For bank name change: Submit request with identity proofs

Mobile Number Not Updated

+

Solution: Ensure the same mobile number is registered with both Aadhaar and your bank account.

  • Update mobile in Aadhaar through OTP or biometric verification
  • Update mobile in bank through written request

Account Not DBT Enabled

+

Solution: Specifically request your bank to enable DBT on your account along with Aadhaar seeding.

  • Visit branch and fill DBT enablement form
  • Ensure CBS (Core Banking System) reflects DBT status

Transaction Failed During Seeding

+

Solution: Try the process again after some time or use alternative methods.

  • Check network connectivity and try again
  • Verify Aadhaar number is entered correctly
  • Contact bank's customer service for assistance
